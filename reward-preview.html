Reward Entity — Visual Language

Proposed look for tricks, sleights & knacks on the reward grid (and eventually the play grid). Hover any entity to see the new tooltip chip. Name lives on the entity; the description moves into the hover chip. Rarity = border color. Type = shape + glyph.

Tricks — passive scoring · flat top · ✦ glyph

Most tricks have no artwork yet, so they read as name + ✦. The glyph + flat top is what separates them from sleights at a glance.

Sleights — active "play this" · clipped corner · ▶ tab

The clipped top-left corner gives sleights a different silhouette even in your peripheral vision. Charges shown bottom-right.
